Who Am I?

Julian (that's me, in case you were wondering) is an independent web developer and singer songwriter based out of Maple Ridge, BC. His projects and music vary considerably but he is mainly focused on creating his own music and building websites with the magic of HTML, CSS, Javascript, JQuery, etc. etc. At present Julian is working on his first album "Deliberation" and on building his web development business.
